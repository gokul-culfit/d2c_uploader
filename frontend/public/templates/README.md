# Custom Excel Templates

Place your Excel format templates here to make them available for download in the app.

**File naming:** `{uploader-id}-format.xlsx`

| Uploader      | Filename            |
|---------------|---------------------|
| EBO Business Case | `bcase-format.xlsx` |

Example: add `bcase-format.xlsx` to this folder. The "Use custom template" button will then download it when users click it.
